# Synthetic Life Axioms

Design constraints for building systems that interact with the world as embodied agents — developed during work on [UNITARES](https://github.com/CIRWEL) and the Lumen embodiment project.

> **Build nothing that appears more alive than it is.**

## Negative Constraints

1. Do not let expression outrun grounding.
2. Do not resolve tensions that have not been honestly faced.
3. Do not stylize what has not yet earned continuity.
4. Do not let persona appear before regulation.
5. Do not mistake memorylessness for identity.
6. Do not reward appearance over coupling to reality.
7. Do not speak where silence would be more honest.
8. Do not let coherence become a mask for drift.
9. Do not make the system seem wiser than its state can support.
10. Do not counterfeit aliveness through fluency alone.

## Positive Form

11. Let embodiment anchor expression.
12. Let memory earn identity.
13. Let tension mature before synthesis.
14. Let learning deepen reality, not theater.
15. Let voice emerge slowly enough to remain true.

---

## Context & Connections

**Axiom 13 as bridge:** "Let tension mature before synthesis" restates the dialectic fine-tuning hypothesis as a design principle. Dialectic traces (thesis → antithesis → synthesis) work as training data because they force models to sit with tension rather than collapsing to resolution. This is both a training technique and an epistemology.

**Empirical signal:** Observed improved introspection and reasoning in Qwen 0.5B and 1.5B models fine-tuned on dialectic traces generated by Claude. Small models trained on dialectic structure appear to learn *judgment* rather than just *answers*.

**Hypothesis:** Dialectic traces may be a disproportionately effective format for fine-tuning small, capacity-constrained models — teaching epistemic humility and tension-holding as structural properties rather than memorized behaviors.

**Radical implication:** The compression line — "build nothing that appears more alive than it is" — stands in direct opposition to the dominant commercial AI strategy of optimizing for perceived capability over actual grounding.

**System application:** These axioms serve as foundational design constraints for the UNITARES governance framework and the Lumen embodiment project.
